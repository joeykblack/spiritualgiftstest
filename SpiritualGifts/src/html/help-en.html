Directions for taking the assessment

LifeThrive’s Gifting Index is comprised of 100 questions in which you will be asked to rank your strength or weakness as it pertains to a particular aspect of each gift. Please read each statement completely. Once you understand the statement rank how closely this applies to you, NOT what you desire or hope for. Assign the score according to the following scale:

5 – I am motivated to act this way regularly.

4 – I am motivated to act this way from time to time.

3 – I experience this on occasion.

2 – This has occurred in my Christian walk, but not normally.

1 – This is a valuable gifting, but my focus is in other areas.
